## Notes

- **References**: Academic references in FAQ and Knowledge Hub articles are kept in English per academic convention.
- **Acronyms**: CASA, WHO, ISO, ESHRE, ASRM are typically retained; local abbreviations (e.g. OMS, ВОЗ, СЗО, DSÖ) may be used for WHO where appropriate.
- **Consistency**: When adding new content, cross-reference this glossary to ensure terminology consistency across all 18 locales.
