About iSperm Medical

Pioneering the Future of Reproductive Medicine

Our Story

iSperm Medical R&D team developed China's first home-use CASA system in 2017, and the first fully automated CASA system in 2018.

Today, we continue to lead the industry with cutting-edge technology like the SQA-900, SQA-6100vet, providing technicians worldwide with the most automated tools for reproductive assessment.

Who We Are

iSperm Medical is a pioneering medical technology company dedicated to advancing reproductive medicine through innovation. We specialize in the integration of microfluidic technology, point-of-care testing (POCT), and AI-driven morphological analysis to deliver smart, efficient, and accessible fertility diagnostic solutions.
